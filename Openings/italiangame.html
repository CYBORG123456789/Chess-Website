Italian game

This opening is played after 1.e4 e5 2.Nf3 Nc6 3.Bc4. The point of this opening is to immediately focus on the weak F pawn which is only defended by the king with moves like knight g5. This is quite a common opening even at the grandmaster level. This is also my favourite opening.
