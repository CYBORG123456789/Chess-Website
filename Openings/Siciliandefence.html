Sicilan defence

The Sicilian Defence is a chess opening that begins with the following moves:1. e4 c5. The point of this is if white pushes d4, Cxd4 and Qxd4 would result in black having two central pawns versus white's one central pawn. This is one of the most thery based openings and is very often played at d grandmaster level.
